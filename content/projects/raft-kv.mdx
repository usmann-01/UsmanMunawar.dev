## The problem

Distributed consensus is the part of systems work I understand least by reading and
most by building. Raft is designed to be understandable, which makes it the right
place to start.

## The approach

The plan is to implement Raft in stages — leader election first, then log
replication, then snapshotting — and only then layer a key-value store on top so the
consensus core stays testable in isolation.

## Where it stands

Planned. Design notes and the build series will land before the first commit.
