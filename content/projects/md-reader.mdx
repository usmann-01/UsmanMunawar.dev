## The problem

I read a lot of Markdown — notes, docs, READMEs — and wanted a native, snappy
reader that stayed out of the way instead of a heavyweight editor.

## The approach

A small desktop shell wraps a rendering pipeline that streams the parsed document
into a virtualized view, so only what's on screen is laid out. Scroll position is
kept in sync between the raw source and the rendered output.

## Where it stands

Complete and in daily use. It's the kind of tool I built for myself first and
then cleaned up enough to share.
