## The problem

Sim racing produces a firehose of telemetry, but the raw numbers don't tell you
*how* to go faster. I wanted something that turned the live stream into actionable
coaching while I'm still on track.

## The approach

The tool listens to the game's telemetry feed, buffers laps, and compares your
current lap against your best. A lightweight overlay surfaces the few tips that
actually matter in the moment — brake later here, get on throttle earlier there.

## Where it stands

In progress. Live parsing and the throttle/brake traces work; the coaching
heuristics and lap-delta overlay are being tuned against real driving data.
